Meet The Cast!

Leo

Leo is a black Siberian-Persian cat who loves to sleep, eat grass, and sit by the laptop during Zoom meetings. He also loves to chase insects and lizards and usually gets crazy zoomies at midnight. He is a very affectionate cat and loves his hooman very much <3

Momo

Momo is a brown Persian cat named after the lemur from Avatar: The Last Airbender. She is a very shy but affectionate cat who loves to hunt and enjoys being outdoors. She occasionally hunts lizrads and cockroaches. Momo spends most of her time sleeping or playing with her son - Gray.

Gray

Gray is one of the five kittens from Leo and Momo's litter. He is a Siberian cat who loves to eat and sleep all day long. He is not very social, but will happily cuddle with hoomans. He loves to play hunt with Momo
